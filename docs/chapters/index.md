personas who use the finished system (Chapters 24–32).

## How to Use This Textbook

Chapters are ordered so that every concept appears after all of its
prerequisites — you can read straight through, or jump to a chapter directly
once you've covered its prerequisite chapters (listed at the bottom of each
chapter page). Part 1 assumes no prior xAPI or IEEE-standards knowledge; Part 2
assumes familiarity with JSON and RESTful HTTP APIs; Part 3's persona chapters
require no additional technical background beyond what Parts 1–2 introduce.

---

**Note:** Each chapter includes a list of concepts covered. Make sure to
complete prerequisites before moving to advanced chapters.
